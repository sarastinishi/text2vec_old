State of the art: http://www.aclweb.org/aclwiki/index.php?title=RG-65_Test_Collection_(State_of_the_art)

RG, consists of 65 pairs of words collected by
Rubenstein and Goodenough (1965), who had them
judged by 51 human subjects in a scale from 0.0 to
4.0 according to their similarity, but ignoring any
other possible semantic relationships that might appear
between the terms
